House Shows

An interface that allows artists to post invitations to local shows they are hosting at small venues. Users can then purchase tickets to these events through the interface. The design is kept simple and functional so it is user-friendly to a large variety of audiences.

Tools and Skills used: Sketch, Angle, Usability Tests, InVision, Mobile-first approach
