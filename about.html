About Avery Zabudsky

Hi! I’m Avery, a creative designer living a humble, off-grid lifestyle on a small island in Ontario, Canada. I thrive on being creative and working in a unique environment. I have experience as a User-Interface Designer, Brand-Identity Specialist, Graphic Designer and Visual Artist. I love creating exclusive pieces to help my clients solidify their brands. I believe in pushing the boundaries to create simple interfaces that have a big impact. I hold formal degrees from recognized institutions in User- Interface Design, Psychology and Visual Design.
